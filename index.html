PERCY JACKSON AND THE BATTLE OF LABYRINTH

Battle of the LABYRINTH. Synopsis: A wonderful and gripping story which succesfully makes us travel far and wide, frequent trips to the land of the dead with a tragic prophecy that can end the world. With the most feared maze of all times -the Labyrinth at his side Percy Jackson,the son of Poseiden-THE GOD OF THE SEA has two breath taking options, to save himself or to save the world. On a suspenseful journey Rick Riordan enthralls us by his stories with a combination of puns, jokes and subtle wit, along side a gripping and truly enhancing story line.
